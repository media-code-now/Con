Kitchen

Kitchen Remodeling

Genesis Construction will help you remodel and repurpose your kitchen, turning it into your favorite room in the house. When it comes to kitchen design, function and efficiency are key. Planning a comfortable layout and adding in a tasteful design is the secret to succeed in creating a beautiful, operating kitchen.

    Things to pay attention to when remodeling your kitchen:
  • Kitchen Islands
  • Cabinet Design
  • Appliances
  • Countertop Selection
  • Lighting Fixtures
  • Flooring Options
  • Kitchen Islands

    • A kitchen with an island provides you with extra space to enhance your cooking skills and entertain company. Adding sinks, electrical outlets or a stove to your island will make it more functional and efficient. Besides the comfort and ease that an island brings to the kitchen, it is also a beautiful aesthetic addition to the heart of the house.

  • Cabinet Design

    • Budgeting and choosing cabinet materials is crucial since they hole a lot of weight from small appliances, pots, pans, dishes, food items and other kitchen accessories. Consider your storage needs and finance in order to choose the best option for your kitchen cabinets. Choosing knobs and handles is also crucial for installation and must be done with quality hardware that complements the entire cabinetry ensemble.

  • Appliances

    • Updated appliances are a big chunck of renovation costs but are essential for a newly styled and energy-efficient kitchen. Not only will your kitchen tasks be easier and faster, they will also save you hundreds of dollars throughout the year thanks to high-efficiency energy technology equipped in new appliances. Just immagine the endless possibilities and joy of cooking with innovative appliances in your newly renovated kitchen.

  • Countertop Selection

    • Durability, maintenance, style and budget are the main things to think about when choosing a countertop material. A wide variety of materials is available to choose from so it's important to choose one that is most durable and can withstand high traffic and spills but also complements your style.

  • Lighting Fixtures

    • Correct lighting can create an inviting atmosphere and an illusion of a larger living space. Since there is such a large variety of options and unlimited designs, Genesis Construction will help you to make the best choice for your home, giving it warmth and comfort.

  • Flooring Options

    • A kitchen floor needs to be durable for high-traffic and spills. Being the heart of the house, a kitchen floor needs to be easy to maintain and clean throughout the years and also compliment the rest of the design. Tile, marble, laminate and engineered wood are all great options but it's best to consult with an expert before installment.
