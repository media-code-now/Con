Painting

A fast, simple way to revive your home.

A professional paint job is one of the easiest and least expensive ways to transform to look of your home. With a fresh coat of paint, you can change interior and exterior walls, freshen up dirty, stained walls, increase the resale value of your home and boost curb appeal.

Interior Painting

Although you clean the rooms in your house on a regular basis, you probably don’t clean your walls. They are forgotten surfaces that don’t serve a daily function, but they still collect dirt, dust, fingerprints, marks, and stains, especially if you have a busy household, small children and pets.Over time, you’ll need a fresh coat of paint to keep your interior clean and bright. Choosing the right color palette and applying unique painting techniques is what we do best.

Making The Right Choice

When it’s time for an interior refreshment, choosing the right paint will give you the best coverage and save you money. High quality paint will be easier to clean and maintain after time. Proper layer application will keep your home looking fresh for years.

Design Tip

If you’re using a dark paint color, a satin or eggshell paint is most recommended. Dark colors have more colorant which boosts shine. If your walls have dents or imperfections, choose a lighter paint color to decrease appearance of defects.

Exterior Painting

Preparation

Planning and preperation is key when painting an exterior wall. Before applying the paint it is essential to sand rough surfaces, patch up holes, dents or cracks and line windows and doors. Correct preperation will prevent future weather damages and withstand harsh conditions.

Painting

Once preperation is complete, applying two coats of paint will assure long-term results. Pay close attention to the type of surface in need of painting in order to choose the correct type of paint and always follow th emanufacturer's directions if applying the paint yourself.
