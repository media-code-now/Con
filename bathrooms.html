Bathroom Remodeling

Correct Planning

Genesis Construction provides free in-home consultations before any planning or design starts. After the assessment of your foundation and starting to build a strategy, it is crucial to plan out the colors, materials and fixtures you want in your bathing space. The colors don't have to match the rest of your house perfectly but do consider details such as door knobs and flooring.

    Things to pay attention to when remodeling your bathroom:
  • Design Trends
  • Vanities
  • Countertop Selection
  • Soaking Tubs
  • Walk-in Showers
  • Design Trends

    • These days a bathroom is a space for one to undwind, relax and refresh themselves. To deliver all these luxuries, a high-end design is essential, especially when it comes to a master bathroom. Genesis Construction provides expert designers that will turn your bathing area in to an oasis.

  • Vanities

    • Free-standing vanities have now replaced the old-fashioned cabinets and are giving bathrooms a clean, sleek appeal. Whichever style you prefer, be it antique-resemblence or innovative luxury, double sinks and a variety of styles are available for custom designs.

  • Soaking Tubs

    • Providing spa-like features, soaking tubs are very popular homeowners. Modern, free-standing styles as well as built-in and sunken styles are available to give your bathroom a unique, relaxing feel. Soaking tubs are also equipped with various water jets and controls to enhance your bathing experience.

  • Walk-in Showers

    • Using top-tier materials is essential when remodeling a shower to ensure lifetime results and easy maintenance. A variety of shower heads is available, as well as luxurious rain-style shower heads which cover the entire walk-in shower bed.
