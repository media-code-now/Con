Landscape

Outdoor Living Area

Creating an inviting, functional and comfortable outdoor living area requires proper planning. Assessing your wants and needs, the size of your back or front yard, landscape conditions and budget limitationg will help you come up with the best plan and provide you with a beautiful place in which you can relax and unwind.

Enjoying the Outdoors

Features such as an outdoor kitchen, a bar, cozy fireplace, pool or jacuzzi and even a zen koi pond are all beautiful additions to your outdoor living area. Take your lifestyle in to consideration when planning these amenities so that you don't end up wasting time and money on luxury items that won't be in much use.

Sizing your Backyard

Determining your outdoor layout can be tricky with certain sizes. If you have limited space, it's best to limit your features to minimize damage to the landscape. Even if there is a larger space to work with, try not to crowd your backyard with features and amenities, but create a comfortable flow with green landscapes and relaxing ambiance.

Design According to Existing Limitations

Having a landscape that matches your design plans is very rare and lucky. Sometimes, when adding features such as a spa, pergola or fire pit, additional preperations may be needed. Plan your backyard design with attention to safety, flowing movement, visual interest and functional amenities.

Entertaining Areas

Outdoor kitchens or bar areas are a great addition to your patio or deck. Providing endless opportunities and various amenities, your outdoor entertaining needs are sure to be fulfilled.
